Loss Definition
robust MC: E_{sigma,tau}[L] (Monte Carlo average)
$$ \begin{aligned} &\textbf{Surrogate output:}\quad t\in\mathbb{R}^{2\times 2\times C},\qquad s=\mathrm{merge}(t)\in\mathbb{R}^{3\times C}\ \ (R,G,B).\\[2pt] &\textbf{Band average:}\quad A_{c,b}=\frac{1}{\lambda_{b,1}-\lambda_{b,0}} \int_{\lambda_{b,0}}^{\lambda_{b,1}} s_c(\lambda)\,d\lambda, \qquad c\in\{R,G,B\}.\\[2pt] &\textbf{In-band / out-of-band:}\quad D_R=A_{R,R},\ D_G=A_{G,G},\ D_B=A_{B,B},\\ &\qquad O_R=\tfrac12(A_{G,R}+A_{B,R}),\ O_G=\tfrac12(A_{R,G}+A_{B,G}),\ O_B=\tfrac12(A_{R,B}+A_{G,B}).\\[4pt] &\textbf{Spectral loss:}\quad L_{\mathrm{ratio}}=\sum_{b\in\{R,G,B\}}\log\frac{O_b+\varepsilon}{D_b+\varepsilon},\quad L_{\mathrm{abs}}=\sum_{b}(1-D_b),\quad L_{\mathrm{oob}}=\sum_{b}O_b,\\ &\qquad L_{\mathrm{purity}}=-\sum_{b\in\{R,G,B\}}\log\left(\frac{A_{b,b}}{\sum_{c\in\{R,G,B\}}A_{c,b}+\varepsilon}+\varepsilon\right),\\ &\qquad L_{\mathrm{spec}}= w_{\mathrm{ratio}}L_{\mathrm{ratio}}+ w_{\mathrm{abs}}L_{\mathrm{abs}}+ w_{\mathrm{oob}}L_{\mathrm{oob}}+ w_{\mathrm{purity}}L_{\mathrm{purity}}.\\[4pt] &\textbf{Regularization (field }u\in[0,1]^{H\times W}\textbf{):}\quad L_{\mathrm{gray}}=\frac{1}{HW}\sum_{i,j} u_{ij}(1-u_{ij}),\quad L_{\mathrm{tv}}=\mathrm{mean}(|\nabla_x u|+|\nabla_y u|),\\ &\qquad L_{\mathrm{reg}}= w_{\mathrm{gray}}L_{\mathrm{gray}}+ w_{\mathrm{tv}}L_{\mathrm{tv}}.\\[4pt] &\textbf{Total (robust MC):}\quad L_{\mathrm{total}}= \mathbb{E}_{(\sigma,\tau)}\big[L_{\mathrm{spec}}+L_{\mathrm{reg}}\big] \ \approx\ \frac{1}{N}\sum_{n=1}^{N}\big(L_{\mathrm{spec}}^{(n)}+L_{\mathrm{reg}}^{(n)}\big). \end{aligned} $$
